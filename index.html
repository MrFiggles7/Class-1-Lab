Mechanical Keyboards

Getting Started

Introduction

The custom mechanical keyboard hobby is a hobby for people that hate themselves and their wallets. It involves spending countless hours scouring the internet for parts that are either A. sold out or B. are only available for pre-order (in which you will spend hundreds of dollars only to wait for months or even years to have it manufactured and delivered.) The result of this hobby is a nice desk aesthetic and some thick keyboard ASMR sounds..
